# Pricing — Finotaur

Finotaur offers two products. You can subscribe to either independently.
All prices are in USD. For the authoritative, always-current pricing, see
https://www.finotaur.com/pricing

---

## Product 1 — Trading Journal

A broker-connected trading journal that auto-imports your real trades and
grades your edge with analytics and an AI coach. Built futures-first with
native Tradovate integration.

### Basic
- Price: $24.99/month, or $229/year ($19.08/month billed annually)
- Free trial: 14 days
- Limits: 25 trades/month, 1 broker connection
- Includes: Automatic broker sync, full Finotaur Academy (300+ lessons),
  full performance analytics & equity curve, strategy builder & playbooks,
  trading sessions & tagging, advanced statistics, risk/reward calculator,
  trade screenshots & notes, email support

### Premium
- Price: $44.99/month, or $409/year ($34.08/month billed annually)
- Free trial: none (paid from day 0)
- Limits: Unlimited trades, multiple broker connections
- Includes: Everything in Basic, plus — Finotaur Score (one number grading
  your real edge), daily AI briefing, Pattern of the Week, Leak Finder
  (AI names the exact mistake costing you money), behavioral & risk alerts,
  custom AI reports & backtesting, priority support, early access to new features

---

## Product 2 — Market Intelligence Platform

AI-driven market research and trading-intelligence terminal: AI stock/sector/
macro/earnings analysis, options-flow scanner, institutional/dark-pool options
data, insider & 13F tracking, and curated trade-idea newsletters.

### Free
- Price: $0
- Includes: AI Stock Analyzer, Top Movers, Earnings Calendar, Watchlists,
  Crypto data, Market News

### Core
- Price: $59/month, or $599/year ($49.92/month billed annually)
- Free trial: 14 days
- Includes: Everything in Free, plus — AI Sector / Macro / Earnings analysis,
  Options Suite, Options Flow Scanner, Institutional Options Flow, Trading Journal access

### Finotaur
- Price: $109/month, or $1,090/year ($90.83/month billed annually)
- Free trial: 14 days
- Includes: Everything in Core, plus — TOP SECRET & WAR ZONE newsletters,
  AI Top 5, AI Options Intelligence, AI Copilot, Block Trades, Trade Copier

### Copilot
- Price: $200/month, or $2,000/year ($166.67/month billed annually)
- Free trial: none
- Includes: Everything in Finotaur, plus — the highest-tier AI Copilot
  experience and priority access
